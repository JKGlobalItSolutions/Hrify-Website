Attendance Management

Automated Daily Attendance Tracking: Automatically record daily attendance, reducing manual tracking efforts and providing accurate data on employee presence.

Work Hours & Overtime Monitoring: Track and manage regular hours, overtime, and break times to improve productivity, ensure compliance, and address any work-hour discrepancies.

Leave Management

Streamlined Leave Requests & Approvals:Employees can submit leave requests with ease, while managers receive instant notifications to approve or reject, ensuring a smooth and timely process.
