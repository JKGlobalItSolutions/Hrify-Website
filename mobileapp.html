HRIFY Employee App - Simplifying HR on the Go

The HRIFY Employee App streamlines payroll, leave, and attendance administration by putting necessary HR tools at workers' fingertips. Employees can easily stay informed and connected because of the dashboard's ease of use.

  • ● Check-in/Check-Out: Simple one-touch check-in and check-out for accurate time tracking.
  • ● Attendance Overview: View attendance history and current status at a glance.
  • ● Leave Tracking: Seamlessly manage leave balances, request leaves, and track approvals.
  • ● Payroll Access: Instantly access payroll details, view salary breakdowns, and download payslips.
