HRIFY BOOSTER Employee App & Web Software

The HRIFY BOOSTER Employee Portal is designed to provide your employees with easy, real-time access to their HR essentials. Whether on the go or at their desks, employees can manage their attendance, view payroll details, submit leave requests, and track performance—all from a secure and intuitive interface. The Employee Portal makes HR tasks simple, transparent, and efficient, giving employees more control over their work-life balance.

User-Friendly Dashboard: The Employee Portal features a clean, intuitive dashboard where employees can easily navigate to their attendance, leave, payroll, and other key HR information. Employees can quickly find and manage their data without confusion.

Lead & Follow-Up Management: Manage potential candidates, track lead progress, and schedule follow-ups from a single interface. Keeps track of recruitment leads and helps optimize the hiring process by ensuring timely follow-ups.

Real-Time Attendance Tracking: Employees can clock in/out, track their work hours, and monitor overtime in real-time via the app or web portal. Ensures accurate work hour tracking and minimizes payroll discrepancies.

Leave Management: Employees can view their leave balance, submit leave requests, and track the status of requests (approved, pending, or rejected). Employees have full visibility and control over their leave, reducing administrative workload.

Payroll & Payslips Access: Payroll & Payslips Access Employees can securely access their payslips, salary breakdowns, and tax information anytime. Promotes transparency and empowers employees to manage their financial details on their own.

Performance & Attendance Reports: View personal attendance records, late entries, and performance reviews in one easy-to-read format. Employees stay informed about their performance and can take proactive steps to improve.

Push Notifications & Alerts: Receive real-time notifications about payroll updates, leave status, attendance records, and important announcements. Keeps employees informed and up-to-date, minimizing communication gaps.

Mobile & Web Access: Mobile & Web Access Accessible on both mobile apps (Android/iOS) and desktop web browsers, ensuring employees can access HR data wherever they are. Flexibility to access HR essentials on the go or at the desk.

Benefits for Employees

Full Control: Employees can manage and access their own data (attendance, leave, payroll) without HR intervention.

Transparency: Employees always know where they stand with leave balances, attendance records, and salary breakdowns.

Efficiency: Streamlined leave requests, payroll access, and attendance tracking reduce the time spent on HR-related tasks.

HRIFY BOOSTER Admin App & Web Software

The HRIFY BOOSTER Admin Portal offers comprehensive tools to manage your workforce efficiently. Admins can oversee employee records, manage attendance, leave approvals, payroll processing, and more—all from one centralized system. With powerful data analytics and reporting features, HRIFY BOOSTER’s Admin Portal streamlines HR workflows, increases transparency, and simplifies compliance.

User Management Admins can add, edit, and remove employee profiles, assign roles, and manage access to the Employee Portal. Simplifies onboarding and ensures that only authorized personnel have access to sensitive HR data.

Lead & Follow-Up Management Manage potential candidates, track lead progress, and schedule follow-ups from a single interface. Keeps track of recruitment leads and helps optimize the hiring process by ensuring timely follow-ups.

Attendance & Time Tracking ManagementAttendance & Time Tracking Management View and approve attendance logs, track employee working hours, and calculate overtime. Ensures compliance with working hour regulations and allows admins to generate accurate payroll reports.

Leave Approval Workflow Admins can approve or deny leave requests from employees based on availability and company policy. Streamlines the leave approval process and prevents staffing issues.

Payroll Processing & Payslip Generation Payroll Processing & Payslip Generation Admins can manage payroll, calculate salaries, generate payslips, and handle tax deductions. Automates complex payroll tasks, reduces human error, and ensures compliance with tax laws.

Analytics & Reporting Generate real-time reports on employee attendance, leave, payroll, and overall HR performance. Admins can make data-driven decisions based on actionable insights and trends.

Employee Management Manage employee records, track performance, and store important documents (contracts, performance reviews, etc). Provides a centralized location for all employee data and enhances HR decision-making.

Tax Compliance & Automated Deductions HRIFY BOOSTER ensures tax deductions are applied automatically based on local laws and company policies. Minimizes the risk of tax-related errors and ensures timely, accurate tax filings.

Mobile & Web Access for Admins Access and manage HR data on-the-go via mobile apps or through the secure web portal. Admins can handle HR responsibilities anytime, anywhere, providing flexibility to work remotely or while traveling.

Benefits for Employees

Streamlined HR Operations Admins can manage the full HR cycle—from recruitment to payroll—with a single, integrated tool.

Increased Accuracy Automation of payroll, tax calculations, and leave tracking reduces errors and saves time.

Actionable Insights Real-time analytics and reporting help HR managers make informed decisions and improve workforce planning.

Centralized HR Data All employee records and documents are stored in one place for easy access and management.
