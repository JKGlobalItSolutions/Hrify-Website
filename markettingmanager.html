Marketing Manager

Job Overview

The Marketing Manager at HRIFY will lead efforts to expand our brand presence, generate new leads, and build marketing strategies that attract potential clients. This role requires a creative thinker with an analytical mindset.

Key Responsibility

Develop and execute marketing campaigns to promote HRIFY products.

Analyze market trends and customer behavior to optimize marketing strategies.

Manage social media, email marketing, and digital advertising efforts.

Collaborate with sales and product teams to align marketing strategies.

Skills Required

Experience in digital marketing and lead generation.

Strong knowledge of SEO, SEM, and social media platforms.

Creativity and an ability to think outside the box.

Excellent communication skills.
